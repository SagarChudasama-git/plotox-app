Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about plotox? We've got answers.

Is my data safe with plotox? expand_more
Yes, 100%. plotox runs entirely client-side. Your uploaded CSV/TSV data is parsed, calculated, and visualized directly in your browser. No files are ever sent to a server.
What chart types are supported? expand_more
We support Line charts, Grouped and Stacked Bar charts, Area charts, Pie and Donut charts, Scatter plots, and Histograms out of the box.
Can I edit my data in the app? expand_more
Yes! In the Data Inspector table, you can double-click any column header to rename it. The renamed keys will instantly apply across all labels, axes selectors, and active legends.
How does the automatic data cleaning work? expand_more
When you upload your dataset, our smart pipeline automatically strips symbols (like $, %, or commas), standardizes date columns to a unified timeline, ignores null or empty entries, and lets you rename columns dynamically.
Can I export charts in high-resolution vector formats? expand_more
Yes! You can export your charts as high-fidelity PNG images, scalable SVG vector graphics (perfect for publications), standalone interactive HTML files, or clean JSON option structures.
Is there a file size limit for CSV uploads? expand_more
Since plotox parses and renders data using high-speed client-side Javascript, it can comfortably handle files with tens of thousands of rows. The only limit is your local computer's memory.
Can I customize chart colors and themes? expand_more
Absolutely! plotox features a sleek, global dark mode toggle that instantly translates your color choices, chart backgrounds, axes lines, labels, legends, and tooltip panels between light and dark modes.
